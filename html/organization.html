Spain

Description

The MPS Spain Association is a non-profit entity that works every day to raise awareness of Lysosomal diseases such as Mucopolysaccharidoses, Fabry Disease and Gaucher Disease among others. Their mission is to provide support to people and families who suffer from these pathologies, improve their quality of life and promote their social integration.
MPS-LISOSMALES España is the leading entity that works for those affected by lysosomal diseases at the national level. The entity is represented by a scientific committee, a medical advisory committee and parents with affected children.

Objectives

· Promote scientific research into the causes, development and therapies for lysosomal diseases.
· Advise, support and provide information to affected families.
· Promote knowledge of these diseases among scientific medical personnel, those affected and the general population, organizing seminars, conferences, congresses and public events.
· Raise awareness in society about the problems of these diseases and the needs of patients and their families.
· Promote prevention, specifically those measures that prevent genetic transmission.
